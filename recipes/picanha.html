Picanha

Picanha Cut after resting

Picanha, known but many as "The Queen" is a traditional way of preparing the Sirloin Cap in Brazil. Its a tender, juicy cut very well known for the delicious fat it carries on top. It has now become very popular around the world and its really easy to prepare, as long as you pay attention to the little details.

The preparation is really straightforward and can be cooked in a grill or a pan. As far as tradition goes, it is expected to be prepared on a charcoal grill. With that in mind, lets start with the recipe.

Ingredients and Tools

Ingredients:

  • Whole Sirloin Cap
  • Salt (Better if coarse)

In case you are wondering: yes, it is that simple.

Tools

  • Grill
  • Charcoal
  • Tongs
  • Knive and Cutting Board
