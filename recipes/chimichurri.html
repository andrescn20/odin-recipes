Chimichurri

If there is a good companion for grilled meat, its chimichurri. Widely used in South America and made famous by Argentinians, chimichurri is perfect for enhancing your steaks flavor withouh interfiring with it. Lets take a look at this simple and magnificent recipe, including an extra optional step for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • Fresh or Dried Parsley
  • Oil (Avocado Oil is preffered)
  • Garlic
  • Apple Vinegar

Preparation

We've got 2 options, lets see both of them.

Option 1 (Fast Method):

  1. Chop the parsley and the Garlic
  2. Mix both ingredients with enough oil to cover twice the volume
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of vinegar at the time and taste until desired acidity
  4. Serve over the grilled meat

Option 2 (Added Flavor):

  1. Chop the parsley
  2. Chop only half of the garlic
  3. Place the rest of the garlic on the grill
  4. Continue with steps 2-4 of Option 1
  5. Place the mix in a metallic bowl and place it in the grill (very low heat preffered)
  6. Once the rest of the garlic is cooked, mashed it and added to the mix.
  7. Slowly stirr the mix every now and then so the flavors infuse the oil
  8. Let it rest with the meat and serve it on top

Also, feel free to add more flavros to the mix, like peppers, lemon or pepper. Get creative with it until you have you perfect chimichurry recipe
